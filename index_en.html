Privacy Policy

"LifeMemory" (hereinafter referred to as "this App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information Collected and Purpose of Use

This App collects the following information for the purposes stated below:

  • Activity Data (steps, calories, etc.) and Sleep Data: Obtained from Health Connect. This data is used solely to visualize your activity history and display it as a life log within the App.
  • Media Files (Photos and Videos): Accessed from your device's storage. This is used to display your memories within the App's "timeline bubble" feature.
  • App Usage Statistics: Collected to analyze your app usage patterns and display them as part of your digital footprint in the life log.

2. Handling of Health Connect Data

This App uses information obtained from Health Connect in accordance with the Health Connect Permissions policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • Data obtained from Health Connect is never used for purposes other than providing and improving App features. It is never used for advertising or sold to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Third-Party Disclosure

All data collected by this App (health data, photos, usage statistics, etc.) is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit this data to any external servers managed by us, nor do we provide it to any third parties.
