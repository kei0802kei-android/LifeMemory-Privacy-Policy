Privacy Policy

"LifeMemory" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. By using the App, users are deemed to have agreed to this policy.

1. Information Collection and Purpose of Use

The App collects the following information for the specified purposes:

  • Healthcare Data (Steps, Sleep, Weight, Calories, etc.): Obtained via Health Connect. This data is used solely to visualize user activity history and display it as a life log.
  • Media Files (Photos, Videos): Accessed locally on the device to display them within the App's diary and reflection features.
  • App Usage Statistics: Obtained based on the "Usage Access" permission. This data is used to analyze the user's own app usage patterns and display them as part of the life log.
  • AI Processing Data: When using AI (Gemini) based analysis or diary generation features, part of the text or statistical data entered by the user is processed in the cloud.

2. Third-Party Services

To improve App quality and deliver advertisements, we use the following third-party services. These services may collect information using cookies or device identifiers.

  • Google AdMob: Uses advertising IDs and other information for displaying and personalizing ads.
  • Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics: Collects anonymous usage statistics and diagnostic information during crashes to help improve the App.
  • Google Generative AI SDK (Gemini): Processes information sent as prompts to generate content via AI.

For the privacy policies of these services, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Strict Handling of Health Connect Data

The App handles information obtained from Health Connect in accordance with Google's "Limited Use Requirements."

  • Healthcare data obtained from Health Connect will never be used for advertising, sold to third parties, used for user identification, or utilized for any other marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

To ensure the protection and transparency of user data, we define our data retention and deletion practices as follows:

  • Retention Period: Life logs (diaries, statistics, settings, etc.) created in the App are stored exclusively on the user's device (locally). This data is retained as long as the user continues to use the App.
  • Manual Deletion: Users can manually delete individual diary entries or custom events through the respective detail screens within the App.
  • Complete Data Deletion: By uninstalling the App, all local data stored on the device will be immediately and permanently deleted.
  • Data on External Services: Data sent for AI (Gemini) processing is not retained on the App developer's servers once the generation process is complete. Anonymous statistics collected by Firebase or AdMob are managed and deleted according to each service's respective policies.

5. Disclaimer

The App is provided "as is." The developer assumes no responsibility for any trouble, loss, or damage resulting from the use of the App (including AI-generated results, data loss, or ad display). Users are responsible for backing up their own important data.
