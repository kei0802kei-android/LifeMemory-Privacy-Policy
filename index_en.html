Privacy Policy

"LifeMemory" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information Collection and Purpose of Use

The App collects the following information for the purposes described below:

  • Healthcare Data (Steps, Sleep, Weight, Calories, etc.): Obtained via Health Connect. This data is used solely to visualize your activity history and display it as a life log within the App.
  • Media Files (Photos and Videos): Accessed to display visual memories within the App's diary and reflection features.
  • App Usage Statistics: Obtained through "Usage Access" permissions. This is used to analyze your app usage patterns and display them as part of your life log.
  • AI Processing Data: When using AI-powered analysis or diary generation features (via Gemini), the text prompts and certain statistical data you provide are processed on cloud servers.

2. Third-Party Services

For quality improvement and ad delivery, the App utilizes the following third-party services. These services may collect information using cookies or device identifiers.

  • Google AdMob (Ad Delivery): Uses information such as the Advertising ID to display and personalize advertisements.
  • Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics (Analysis & Improvement): Collects anonymous usage statistics and diagnostic information to help improve the App's performance and stability.
  • Google Generative AI SDK (Gemini): Processes information sent as prompts to generate AI content.

For more details, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

3. Strict Handling of Health Connect Data

The App's use of information received from Health Connect will adhere to the Health Connect Permissions Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • We do not use data obtained from Health Connect for advertising, third-party sales, user identification, or any other marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Export

The life logs created within the App (diaries, statistics, settings, etc.) are, in principle, stored locally on your device.

  • If you choose to use the "Backup (JSON Export)" feature, data will be written to a location you specify. Managing the security of these exported files is the user's responsibility.

5. Disclaimer

The App is provided "as is." The developer shall not be liable for any trouble, loss, or damage resulting from the use of the App (including AI-generated results, data loss, or ad display). Users are encouraged to maintain their own backups of important data.

6. Open Source Licenses

The App utilizes various open-source libraries, including but not limited to:

  • Android Jetpack (Compose, Room, DataStore, Health Connect, WorkManager, etc.)
  • Google Firebase SDK
  • Google Play Services (Ads, App Update, Review)
  • Google Media3 (ExoPlayer)
  • Google Generative AI SDK
