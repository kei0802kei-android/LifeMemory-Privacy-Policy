LifeMemory User Guide

Last Updated: February 2024

Main Screen Overview

When you open the app, your "Today" is recreated in real-time.

*Screens are under development

Living Avatar

Your avatar mirrors your actions. If you're listening to music, the avatar wears headphones. If you're walking, the avatar starts walking too.

24-Hour Activity Chart

The pie chart in the top right shows your daily activity breakdown (Sleep, Music, Steps, Apps) at a glance.

Memories Timeline

Swipe the center timeline left or right to travel through time. Photos taken at that moment will pop up as "bubbles."

Activity Cards

Tap the panels below to check detailed data for each category.

🏃 Steps
Sync with Health Connect to track distance and calories burned.
⚖️ Weight
Visualize weight trends and track progress toward your goals.
🌙 Sleep
Analyze sleep stages (Deep, Light, REM) in detail.
📱 Digital Well-being
Check how much time you spend on SNS and media apps.
📖 AI Diary
Gemini AI reflects on your day and writes a gentle diary entry for you.

Reflecting on your past self
makes your tomorrow even brighter.
