LifeMemory User Guide

Last Updated: March 2026

Before You Start

To get the most out of LifeMemory, please ensure the following permissions are granted.

Health Connect Integration

Required to sync "Steps," "Sleep," and "Weight." Allowing "All Permissions" ensures seamless integration with apps like Google Fit.

Usage Access

Required for the "Entertainment" card to track app usage time. Please find LifeMemory in your Android settings and toggle "Allow."

Using the Main Screen

When you open the App, your "Today" is recreated in real-time.

*UI is subject to change

Animated Avatar!

If you're listening to music, your avatar wears headphones. If you're walking, the avatar walks too. Expressions change based on your actions.

24-Hour Activity Graph

The circular chart at the top-right visually represents your daily activity (Sleep, Music, Walking, App Usage). See where your time goes at a glance.

Memory Timeline

Swipe left or right on the central timeline to travel back in time. Photos taken at that moment pop up as "speech bubbles."

Activity Cards

Tap the panels below to view detailed data for each category.

🔥 Calories
Estimates calories burned based on activity and profile.
🏃 Steps
Syncs with Health Connect to record distance and daily steps.
⚖️ Weight
Visualize weight trends with graphs to stay on track.
🌙 Sleep
Analyzes sleep stages (Deep, Light) from the previous night.
📱 Entertainment
Monitor your digital well-being by checking app usage time.
📖 Diary
Gemini AI reflects on your day and writes a personal diary for you.
🏆 Collection
Check your earned titles and all-time personal records.
📊 Report
A detailed AI-driven analysis of how you spent your time.

Recording Custom Events

Beyond digital logs, you can manually record "real-life actions" on the timeline. *Tap the timeline to register.

Quick Selection

Easily log common events like Meals, Work, or Exercise by tapping icons. You can also use your favorite emojis!

Coloring the Timeline

Registered events appear as colored bands on the timeline, making it easy to see what you were doing at any time.

Recurring Tasks

For daily routines like "Work" or "Sleep," enable "Recurring Tasks" to automatically display them on future timelines.

Tips for Better AI Diaries

Record the "Small Things"

The more events you register—like "Had a great lunch" or "Went for a walk"—the more specific and emotional the AI-generated diary becomes.

Keeping Your Memories Safe

Data Stays Local

Your diaries and health data are stored only on your device. Your privacy is fully protected and never leaked to external servers.

Backup (JSON Export)

You can save your records as a JSON file via Settings. We recommend regular backups for device changes or peace of mind.
